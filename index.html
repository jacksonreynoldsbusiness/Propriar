The challenge

Owning a French property
from abroad is stressful

You bought a dream. But between the language barrier, unreliable contractors, and the impossibility of being in two places at once — it can feel like anything but.

01
You can't be there
A leak, a broken shutter, a neighbour complaint. By the time you hear about it, it's already a crisis. Distance turns every small problem into an enormous one.
02
The language barrier
French artisans, mairies, insurance companies, notaires — navigating French bureaucracy requires fluency, patience, and local relationships you simply don't have.
03
Nothing is coordinated
You're managing a cleaner, a gardener, a plumber, and a rental agent across different threads. No single person is responsible for making it all work.
